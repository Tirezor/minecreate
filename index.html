MineCreate - is a fan-made Minecraft video game. Created by an anonymous user from the Internet Tirezor. He was inspired by the work of Markus "Notch" Persson, and decided to also create and develop a similar video game. It's a 3D sandbox with a infinite randomly procedurally generated world. World consists of blocks that the player can place and destroy. There are two gamemodes, creative mode and survival mode. In creative mode, the player is immortal and has unlimited resources to build, while in survival mode, the player can die, must mine resources and survive. The project in the Alpha stage of development, and Tirezor is actively developing.
